Top Three Facts of the Roman Empire

Romans invaded land all over the world

You may have heard people say ‘the Roman Empire’ before. Well they had an empire because they didn’t stay in Rome, they went across the world invading different countries and making them part of their empire.

We still use some Roman roads

The Romans built loads and loads of roads in the UK, 55,000 miles actually. Some of these roads are still around today!

Gladiator Fights

That’s where they put people in a ring and they fight to the death! This would happen in huge stadiums (similar to football stadiums now) and was the most famous sport of the day.
